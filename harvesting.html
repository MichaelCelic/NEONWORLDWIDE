Human Harvesting Program

The brain makes for a better battery. Become a harvester, become useful.

Program Overview

The Human Harvesting Program represents the pinnacle of neural processing technology. Volunteers participate in a revolutionary program where their neural activity is harnessed to process massive amounts of data for NEON WORLDWIDE's corporate operations.

Participants are provided with specialized neural interface helmets that connect directly to their brain's neural pathways. While in a comfortable, controlled environment, volunteers' brains process complex computational tasks that would take traditional systems years to complete.

How It Works

Step 1: Application

Submit your application through our secure system. Our recruitment team will review your profile and neural compatibility.

Step 2: Evaluation

Qualified candidates undergo comprehensive neural assessment to determine processing capacity and compatibility with our systems.

Step 3: Integration

Selected volunteers are fitted with our advanced neural interface helmets and integrated into the processing network.

Step 4: Contribution

Volunteers contribute their neural processing power while resting in comfortable facilities, becoming an essential part of corporate operations.

Benefits of Participation

  • • Purpose: Contribute to the advancement of technology and corporate efficiency
  • • Comfort: State-of-the-art facilities designed for optimal comfort during processing
  • • Compensation: Competitive compensation packages for qualified volunteers
  • • Efficiency: Your brain processes data faster and more efficiently than traditional systems
  • • Usefulness: Transform your neural activity into valuable corporate resources

Requirements

Ideal candidates must be of legal age, in good health, and willing to commit to the program. All participants undergo thorough medical and neural compatibility screening. The program is entirely voluntary, and participants can withdraw at any time (subject to contractual terms).
